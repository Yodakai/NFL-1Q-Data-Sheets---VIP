Stat Glossary
Adj_Eff – 1Q efficiency adjusted for opponent.
Success% – % of 1Q plays that stay on schedule.
Explosive% – % of 1Q plays gaining chunk gains (15+ pass, 10+ rush).
Neutral Pass% – 1Q pass rate in neutral script.
aDOT – average depth of target.
YAC/att – yards after contact (rush) or catch (rec).
Carries – average 1Q carry volume.
YPRR – yards per route run.
TPRR – targets per route run.
Catch% vs Exp – catch rate vs expected.
Defense metrics – 0–100 difficulty score; higher = tougher.

Grade & Color Guide
A / A− (Elite, green) – explosive, efficient 1Q usage vs beatable matchups; these are our highest-confidence 1Q OVER spots when price is fair.
B range (Edge, blue) – strong underlying 1Q profile or matchup edge with a little more variance; good candidates for overs when lines are reasonable.
C range (Steady, yellow) – solid but not dominant 1Q production or tougher defenses; more “floor” than “ceiling” and better for context than for auto-bets.
D (High-Risk, red‑orange) – volatile or thin 1Q sample, role concerns, or tough matchup; can still pop, but only for aggressive or correlated builds.
F (Volatile, red) – poor or inconsistent 1Q production into strong defenses or unclear roles; best treated as stay-aways for 1Q OVERs in most cases.
