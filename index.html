Stat Glossary
Adj_Eff – 1Q efficiency adjusted for opponent.
Success% – % of 1Q plays that stay on schedule.
Explosive% – % of 1Q plays gaining chunk gains (15+ pass, 10+ rush).
Neutral Pass% – 1Q pass rate in neutral script.
aDOT – average depth of target.
YAC/att – yards after contact (rush) or catch (rec).
Carries – average 1Q carry volume.
YPRR – yards per route run.
TPRR – targets per route run.
Catch% vs Exp – catch rate vs expected.
Defense metrics – 0–100 difficulty score; higher = tougher.

Grade & Color Guide
A / A− (Elite – green, badge badge-elite) – explosive, efficient 1Q usage vs beatable matchups; our highest-confidence 1Q OVER spots when price is fair.
B range (Edge – blue, badge badge-edge) – strong 1Q profile or matchup edge with a bit more variance; good over candidates at the right number.
C range (Steady – yellow, badge badge-steady) – solid but not dominant 1Q output or tougher defenses; more “floor” than “ceiling,” best as context or supporting angles.
D (High-Risk – red with orange tint, badge badge-highrisk) – thin or volatile 1Q sample, usage doubts, or hard matchup; only for aggressive or correlated builds, not core overs.
F (Volatile – red, badge badge-volatile) – poor or inconsistent 1Q production into strong defenses or unclear roles; usually stay-away territory for 1Q OVERs.
