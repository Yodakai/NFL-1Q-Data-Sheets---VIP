Stat Glossary
Adj_Eff – 1Q efficiency adjusted for opponent.
Success% – % of 1Q plays that stay on schedule.
Explosive% – % of 1Q plays gaining chunk gains (15+ pass, 10+ rush).
Neutral Pass% – 1Q pass rate in neutral script.
aDOT – average depth of target.
YAC/att – yards after contact (rush) or catch (rec).
Carries – average 1Q carry volume.
YPRR – yards per route run.
TPRR – targets per route run.
Catch% vs Exp – catch rate vs expected.
Defense metrics – 0–100 difficulty score; higher = tougher.

Grade & Color Guide
A / A− (Elite, green) – explosive, efficient 1Q usage vs beatable matchups; highest-confidence 1Q OVER spots when price is fair.
B range (Edge, blue) – strong 1Q profile or matchup edge with some variance; good over candidates at reasonable lines.
C range (Steady, yellow) – solid but not dominant 1Q production or tougher defenses; more floor than ceiling, better for context than auto-bets.
D (High-Risk, red-orange) – volatile or thin sample, role concerns, or difficult matchup; only for aggressive or correlated builds.
F (Volatile, red) – poor/inconsistent 1Q production into strong defenses or murky roles; usually stay-aways for 1Q OVERs.
